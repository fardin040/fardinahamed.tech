Who I Am

I'm Md Fardin Ahamed, an Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering student with a genuine passion for Cybersecurity and Computer Networking.

My path began with low‑level electronics, then naturally progressed into understanding how signals become data, how data becomes networks, and how those networks become both targets and fortresses. I find that intersection endlessly fascinating.

I spend my time learning protocols, writing Python scripts, analysing packet captures, and working through CTF challenges. I believe in learning by doing and in openly sharing what I discover along the way.
